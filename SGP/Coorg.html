Destination

Ooty

Ooty, officially known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station nestled in the Nilgiri Hills of the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It's often referred to as the "Queen of Hill Stations" and is celebrated for its enchanting natural beauty and cool, pleasant climate. Ooty is characterized by lush tea gardens, dense forests, and a plethora of scenic spots, including the Ooty Lake, Botanical Gardens, Rose Garden, and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Mysore

Mysore, a charming city in the southern state of Karnataka, India, is renowned for its rich history, cultural heritage, and grand palaces. The city's crown jewel is the Mysore Palace, a magnificent architectural masterpiece that illuminates with thousands of lights during the grand Dasara festival.

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal, often referred to as the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a serene and picturesque hill station located in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Nestled in the Western Ghats, Kodaikanal is known for its lush green landscapes, misty hills, and cool climate. The town is a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts, offering beautiful spots like the Kodaikanal Lake, Coaker's Walk, and the mesmerizing Pillar Rocks

Abby Fall

Abbey Falls, located near Madikeri in the picturesque hill station of Coorg, Karnataka, is a breathtaking natural wonder that draws visitors with its sheer beauty and serenity. The falls are situated in a lush coffee estate, surrounded by dense forests and verdant plantations, making it an idyllic spot for nature enthusiasts and photographers.
