Destination

Gulmarg

Nestled in the Himalayas, Gulmarg offers year-round beauty and adventure. Enjoy skiing in winter, hiking in summer, and stunning views via the Gulmarg Gondola.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam, a picturesque town in Jammu and Kashmir, welcomes you with lush valleys, the glistening Lidder River, and a serene ambiance. It's a haven for trekkers and nature enthusiasts, with verdant landscapes that are perfect for relaxation and exploration.

Srinagar

Srinagar, the heart of Kashmir, enchants with its serene Dal Lake, Mughal gardens, and vibrant markets. Explore historic houseboats and experience the rich culture of this city, often called the "Venice of the East."

Sonmarg

Sonmarg, an alpine wonderland in Jammu and Kashmir, captivates visitors with its rugged landscapes, pristine lakes, and thrilling hiking trails. Embrace the serenity of this high-altitude retreat and revel in its natural beauty.
