Destination

Munnar

Munnar's blend of stunning landscapes, rich culture, and opportunities for adventure make it a captivating hill station that continues to draw travelers from around the world. Whether you're a nature lover, a photography enthusiast, or simply seeking a tranquil escape, Munnar promises a memorable and revitalizing experience.

Thiruvananthampuram

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of the southern Indian state of Kerala, is a city that beautifully marries tradition with modernity, offering a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. This coastal city, formerly known as Trivandrum, is renowned for its laid-back charm and is a treasure trove of experiences for travelers.

Periyar

Periyar, situated in the idyllic landscapes of the Western Ghats in Kerala, is a sanctuary of exceptional natural beauty and rich biodiversity. Known for its lush forests, serene lake, and abundant wildlife, Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is a cherished destination for those seeking a rendezvous with nature.

Backwaters

Kerala's backwaters, an intricate network of lagoons, lakes, and canals, are a unique and mesmerizing natural wonder. These tranquil waterways wind their way through lush, green landscapes, offering a serene and picturesque escape.
