Destination

Dwarka

Dwarka, a sacred city on the western coast of India in the state of Gujarat, is a place of great religious significance. Known as the "Kingdom of Lord Krishna," it is believed to be the legendary capital of Lord Krishna's empire. Dwarka is a place of pilgrimage for millions of devotees, with its temples, including the Dwarkadhish Temple, drawing worshippers from all over the world.

Dholavira

Dholavira is an archaeological site located in the Kutch district of Gujarat, India. It holds immense historical importance as one of the five largest and most prominent cities of the ancient Indus Valley Civilization. This well-preserved archaeological site offers a glimpse into the advanced urban planning and engineering of its time.

Mandvi

Mandvi is a coastal town situated in the Kutch district of Gujarat, India. It is renowned for its beautiful beaches, historical landmarks, and vibrant local culture. The town boasts a rich maritime history and was once a significant port of call for the Indian Ocean trade routes.

Rann of Kutch

The Rann of Kutch, located in the western Indian state of Gujarat, is a unique and captivating natural wonder. This vast salt marsh is a surreal landscape, particularly during the dry season when the ground appears to stretch endlessly, covered in a brilliant white crust
