Destination

Jodhpur

Jodhpur, the "Blue City" of Rajasthan, India, is a captivating destination renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and striking architecture. The city's skyline is dominated by the formidable Mehrangarh Fort, perched atop a rocky hill

Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, often referred to as the "Golden City," is a desert jewel in the heart of Rajasthan, India. Its crowning glory is the majestic Jaisalmer Fort, a massive sandstone structure rising from the desert, with intricate architecture and a rich history.

Jaipur

Jaipur, the "Pink City" of Rajasthan, India, is a city that exudes regal charm and vibrant culture. Known for its historic palaces, colorful markets, and stunning architecture, Jaipur offers a myriad of photogenic opportunities

Bikaner

Bikaner's streets are lined with stunning havelis, each adorned with ornate frescoes and intricate carvings, making them perfect subjects for photography. The city is also famous for the Karni Mata Temple, known as the "Rat Temple," where thousands of rats are revered and provide a unique photo opportunity
