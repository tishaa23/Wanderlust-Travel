Destination

Ganga

The Ganges, revered as the holiest river in India, is a living testament to the spiritual and cultural significance of Varanasi. Flowing gracefully through the city, it offers a mesmerizing spectacle of devotion, rituals, and everyday life

Rishikesh

Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, Rishikesh is a serene and spiritual haven that beckons to seekers and adventurers alike. Known as the 'Yoga Capital of the World,' this quaint town on the banks of the holy Ganges River offers a unique blend of tranquility and exhilaration.

Rudraprayag

Rudraprayag is a picturesque town where the Alaknanda and Mandakini Rivers meet, forming the holy Ganges. This confluence, known as the 'Sangam,' is a sacred site for Hindus and sets the stage for a serene and spiritual experience. Surrounded by lofty peaks and lush greenery, Rudraprayag is a gateway to several pilgrimage destinations, including Badrinath and Kedarnath.

Kashi Vishwanath

Kashi Vishwanath, often referred to as the "Golden Temple," is the spiritual heart of Varanasi and one of the most revered Hindu temples in India. This iconic shrine is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is an embodiment of devotion and architectural grandeur.
